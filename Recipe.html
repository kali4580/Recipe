Buldak Carbonara Cup Noodles

Story About Buldak Carbonara

Buldak Carbonara was launched in December 2017 as a limited edition flavor to celebrate Samyang selling one billion Hot chicken Buldak flavor but it accidentally became very popular. Buldak Carbonara was inspired by the popular online hacks people have been doing by mixing the original Hot chicken buldak flavor with milk and cheese to make the taste less spicy, so Samyang combined those flavors into a pink packaging creating a viral sensation that regularly blows up on the internet.

How to make Buldak Carbonara Cup Noodles

What you need

  • The Buldak Carbonara itself
  • A Fork
  • Hot water

How to make it

  1. Prep the cup by peeling the lid halfway and take out the spicy sauce packet and the cheese powder packet
  2. Add hot water into the cup until it reaches the fill line inside the bowl
  3. Close the lid and let the noodles cook for about 4 minutes
  4. Poke 3 holes on the lid and drain all the water out
  5. Tear the whole lid off and open both the spicy sauce packet and the cheese powder packet and pour it into the noodles
  6. Mix the spicy sauce with the noodles
  7. Add the cheese powder packet and mix it
  8. Enjoy!
